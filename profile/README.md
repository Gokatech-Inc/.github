## About Gokatech Inc

**Gokatech Inc** is a premier AI solutions and IT consulting firm headquartered in **Frisco, Texas**. We partner with organizations at every stage — from ambitious startups to Fortune 500 enterprises — to design, build, and deploy technology that creates a lasting competitive advantage.

Our engineering teams operate across the full stack: from intelligent AI agents and data pipelines to cloud-native platforms and mobile applications. We don't just write code — we architect systems that scale, adapt, and perform in production.

With a global delivery model and deep domain expertise across **finance, healthcare, real estate, retail, automotive, and telecom**, we bring precision engineering and business-aware thinking to every engagement.

> Trusted by **Toyota · Wells Fargo · CVS · Deloitte · Verizon · AT&T · PepsiCo · United Healthcare · Vanguard** and 60+ leading enterprises worldwide.
