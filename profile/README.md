## Who We Are

**Gokatech Inc** is a premier IT consulting and software development firm headquartered in Frisco, Texas. We transform business ideas into impactful technological solutions — serving startups and Fortune 500 companies alike across finance, healthcare, real estate, and retail.

With a global delivery model and U.S.-based leadership, we bring together domain expertise and cutting-edge technology to deliver agility, productivity, and optimized IT costs for our clients. Our team has supported organizations including Toyota, Wells Fargo, CVS, Deloitte, Verizon, AT&T, PepsiCo, United Healthcare, and Vanguard.
